Mission & Vision

Kyun Banaya Yeh
Platform?

Shahkot mein hazaron shops aur businesses hain lekin unhe dhundhna mushkil tha. Kisi ki tamam details online asaani sy nahi milti, koi phone number asaani se nahi milta tha.

Isliye humne Shahkot Directory banaya — taake har insaan apne ghar baithe apne shehar ki koi bhi shop ya service asaani se dhundh sake.

  • Sab businesses ki free digital service dena
  • Users ko fast aur reliable information dena
  • Shahkot ki economy ko support karna
  • Community ko ek platform par jorna
